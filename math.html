Greatest Common Factor

Before you start trying to find the factor of your equation your going to need to find the greatest common factor for the equation.

Examples:
(12a * 3a * 18b * 6ab)/*Both 3 & a are factors of all some/all the numbers which means we can take them out*/
Answer: 3a(4 * 1 * 18b * 2b) /*You can check to see if your answer is correct by distributing it out again!*/
